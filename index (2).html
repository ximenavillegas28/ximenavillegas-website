Light exposure is something I struggled with in the beginning, I practice everyday. I wrote down the time of day of what light exposure setting I should choose based on the season and time of day. I practice shots at night and saw how exposure was affected at night and how to fix my settings on my camera. I got familiar with every setting of my camera and what it did.
